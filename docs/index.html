Privacy Policy for BrewWater Effective date: April 24, 2026 BrewWater is a coffee brew-water recipe and water profile app. We aim to collect as little information as possible. Information you create in BrewWater, such as recipes, water profiles, and app preferences, is stored locally on your device and used only to provide the app’s core functionality. BrewWater does not currently use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or cross-app tracking, and we do not sell your personal information. If you contact us by email, we will receive your email address and any other information you include in your message. We use that information only to respond to you, provide support, and maintain related correspondence as reasonably necessary. You can delete your in-app data at any time from within the app or by removing the app from your device. We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
