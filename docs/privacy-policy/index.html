BrewWater

Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 24, 2026

Overview

BrewWater is a coffee brew-water recipe and water profile app. We aim to collect as little information as possible.

Information Stored on Your Device

Information you create in BrewWater, such as recipes, water profiles, and app preferences, is stored locally on your device and used only to provide the app’s core functionality.

Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

BrewWater does not currently use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or cross-app tracking, and we do not sell your personal information.

If You Contact Us

If you contact us by email, we will receive your email address and any other information you include in your message. We use that information only to respond to you, provide support, and maintain related correspondence as reasonably necessary.

Deleting Your Data

You can delete your in-app data at any time from within the app or by removing the app from your device.

Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date above.
